# PT Trafas Dwi Medika (TDM) — Content Pack for Pitch Deck "IMPACT 2.0"

## 1. Quick Overview

PT Trafas Dwi Medika (TDM) is an Indonesian medical device manufacturer specializing in orthopedic implants, particularly in trauma, spine, and craniomaxillofacial (CMF) applications.

TDM acts as an industrial partner bridging academic research and real-world medical product commercialization.

---

## 2. Positioning Statement

Engineering-driven medical device manufacturer focused on transforming research into clinically applicable orthopedic implant products.

---

## 3. Key Roles in Innovation

TDM is not just a manufacturer, but a **co-developer and commercialization partner**.

### Core Roles:
- Design refinement based on research outputs
- Prototype manufacturing
- Engineering iteration and optimization
- Clinical readiness preparation
- Production scaling and commercialization

## 4. Collaboration Track Record (UI)

TDM has collaborated with Universitas Indonesia (FTUI & FKUI) in multiple research-based product developments.

### Key Experience:
